Session 3: Process Improvement & AI Adoption

Building an AI-Augmented QA Practice

28th July 2026 • 2 Hours • Strategy + Final Presentations

🎯 Learning Objectives

  • Assess your team's QA maturity and identify high-value AI integration points
  • Define AI skills your team needs and create a development path
  • Design guardrails and governance frameworks for AI use in QE
  • Build a business case and phased adoption roadmap
  • Present a complete AI-augmented project demonstration

📊 QA Maturity Spectrum

Level 1: MANUAL       → Ad-hoc testing, no standards, reactive
Level 2: AUTOMATED    → Scripted tests, CI integration, team standards
Level 3: AI-AUGMENTED → AI assists human work, spec-driven, guardrails
Level 4: AI-NATIVE    → AI plans & executes, human sets goals only

Key insight: Don't jump levels. AI works best when there's structure to augment.

🔒 Data Privacy Quick Reference

  • Can share: Open-source patterns, generic logic, synthetic data, anonymized descriptions
  • Cannot share: Proprietary client code, real customer data, PII, credentials, NDA-covered requirements
  • Rule: When in doubt — anonymize or synthesize. Never assume it's safe.
